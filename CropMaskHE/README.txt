These images are from DOI: 10.1038/s41588-024-01819-2

We leveraged the full resolution H&E images of human tissue sections used in Franzen et al., 2024  https://doi.org/10.1038/s41588-024-01819-2 from their data repository (BioStudies S-BSST1410): https://www.ebi.ac.uk/biostudies/studies/S-BSST1410. 

Details of the conversion to binary masks with ImageJ and csv files for NetLogo are available in this document: https://docs.google.com/document/d/1gLd3WoA16utdU47IRkUQjMt5ICv3BE7hrGnWhim46kk/edit?usp=sharing

The file Key-ExtendedDataFig1a.png is a screenshot of Extended Data Fig. 1a of the manuscript, which shows how the file names correspond to patients and categories of IPF severity.
